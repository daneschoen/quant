Top 10 Coin Volatilities

Hold down and rotate the 3-D chart to view from different angles; e.g., 2-D side view, overhead view, etc.
Scroll over the chart to zoom in and out.

This 3-D chart represents the volatility of the top ten cryptocoins.

Volatility is how much the price goes up AND down, indicating the erratic and unstable nature of the asset. The more erratic the movement of price, the higher is the volatility. In contrast, a smoothly moving asset would have low volatility. High volatility in general is bad for investment.

This chart shows that the order of magnitude of movement is similar for most coins, with EOS currently having the highest volatility.
